# IEEE CASS Internship 2026

## Overview

This repository showcases my work completed as part of the **IEEE Circuits and Systems Society (CASS) Internship 2026**. Throughout the internship, I gained hands-on experience in digital system design, Verilog HDL, simulation, verification, and the fundamentals of VLSI design by completing practical assignments and implementing digital circuits.

---

## Internship Goals

- Understand the complete digital IC design workflow.
- Design and implement digital circuits using Verilog HDL.
- Perform simulation and functional verification.
- Learn synthesis and timing analysis concepts.
- Gain practical exposure to FPGA and VLSI design methodologies.
